Hire a Technical Coach

Technical skills have to be acquired deliberately, and the best effect comes from lifting the entire team at the same time. Members of the Samman Technical Coaching Society offer three kinds of coaching.

SAMMAN COACHING FOR TEAMS

The classic format: a block of 10 Samman sessions for a dev team that works together, combining learning hours and ensemble working.
