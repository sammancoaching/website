# Samman Technical Coaching Society Supporters

If you benefit from the work of the society, we encourage you to consider becoming a supporter.  Supporters pay a little bit each month on [Emily Bache's Patreon](https://www.patreon.com/EmilyBache).

Supporters also get access to additional materials and benefits, in the same way as [Contributors]({% link society/contributors/index.md %})
